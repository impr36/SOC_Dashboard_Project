Timestamp Reconstruction

The platform reconstructs attacks chronologically using correlated timestamps collected from process execution, authentication logs, registry activity, network packets, firewall events, and system behavior. This allows analysts to visualize the complete attack progression from initial compromise to persistence and lateral movement.

MITRE ATT&CK Mapping

Detected behaviors are mapped against standardized MITRE ATT&CK techniques including execution, persistence, credential access, privilege escalation, discovery, exfiltration, and command & control activity. This enables structured attack classification and investigation consistency across all analysts.

Incident Reporting & Analyst Workflow

The platform allows analysts to generate structured investigation reports for each correlated incident group. Reports include analyst observations, incident metadata, attack timelines, containment actions, and recommended remediation steps. All reports are stored in the case database and can be exported as PDF or JSON for compliance and post-incident review.
